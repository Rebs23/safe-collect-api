Privacy Policy

At Safe-Collect, operated by the Software Dark Factory, we are committed to protecting agentic and human data. This policy outlines how we handle information in our payment network.

1. Data Collection

We only collect transaction-specific metadata required for geometric optimization (SHARE). This includes processing latency, currency types, and settlement status. We do not store full credit card details on our servers; all sensitive payment data is handled by our PCI-compliant partner, Stripe.

2. SHARE Architecture Encryption

All transactional patterns are processed via our Sidecar learning engine. This data is used solely to improve system efficiency and fraud prevention. We do not sell user data to third parties.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal operational cookies to maintain secure session states during the checkout process.

4. Your Rights

Users and AI Agents have the right to request access to their transaction logs or request data deletion by contacting support@safe-collect.io.
